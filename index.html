How to play

Click on a card to flip it over and see the color on the other side, try to find the other card that matches its color. Match all pairs to completed the game. Only two cards can be flipped at a time.

Try to complete the game in the shortest amount of time with the least amount of moves.

You can restart the game at any time by pressing the restart button.

Good luck!
